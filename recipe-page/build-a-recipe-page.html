Cook Like a Krusty Krab Chef: Krabby Patty Guide

Welcome to the ultimate guide for making the one and only Krabby Patty! While we can't promise access to the Secret Formula (Plankton’s still working on that), this recipe will get you close enough to make even Squidward smile. Crispy edges, juicy patties, and cartoon-level flavor — it's time to cook like you're at the Krusty Krab!

Ingredients

  • 200g ground beef (80/20 fat ratio for juiciness)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce (for extra umami)
  • A pinch of paprika (optional, for flavor & color)
  • A tiny splash of mustard (optional, adds tang)
  • A few breadcrumbs (optional, for a softer texture)

Instructions

  1. Form your ground beef into two small, thin balls.
  2. Smash them onto a hot skillet, then season with salt, pepper, and your spices.
  3. Flip when crispy, add cheese on top, and let it melt.
  4. Butter your buns and toast until golden.
  5. Stack patties on the bottom bun, then add lettuce, tomato, pickles, onions, and your favorite sauce.
  6. Add the top bun and serve immediately — SpongeBob style!
